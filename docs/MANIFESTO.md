# Vibe Engineering Manifesto

## Power without principles breeds chaos.

AI makes creation cheap. It does not make judgment automatic.

The danger is not that people without traditional engineering credentials can build. The danger is that speed can hide weak assumptions, inaccessible design, fragile architecture, unsafe data practices, and permanent platform dependence.

Vibe Engineering exists to preserve the freedom of AI-assisted creation while restoring discipline to the process.

## Freedom without ownership is just another dependency.

A customer should not need permission to access the thing they paid to create.

Our default position:

- the customer controls the agreed source code,
- the customer controls their data,
- the customer controls production accounts and credentials,
- the customer receives documentation and export paths,
- vendors remain replaceable where reasonably possible,
- pricing reflects human time and compute rather than manufactured captivity.

## Engineering is a set of promises

Engineering is not a title reserved for people who memorize every implementation detail. It is a discipline of making promises visible and proving that they hold.

Those promises include:

- the product does what it says,
- risks are not hidden,
- failure is anticipated,
- recovery is possible,
- other people can understand the work,
- the owner remains free.

## Taste is not decoration

Taste is the ability to remove what does not belong, make the important obvious, respect people’s attention, and finish every visible decision.

Taste cannot excuse weak reliability or security. Reliability cannot excuse a confusing product. Vibe Engineering judges the whole system.

## The final measure

We do not measure success by how much an AI generated.

We measure how much reliable freedom the owner gained.
