# Asc3nd — Commercial and Mission Outcome Contract

## Client state

Asc3nd is the first confirmed paying external PAULI STUDIO / MACS client in the current five-lane proving model.

Because payment has already occurred, the primary proof target is **delivery value**, not another sale.

## Locked success metric

Within 30–60 days, prove one real acquisition-to-engagement loop:

1. A real person discovers Asc3nd through a public channel.
2. They understand who Asc3nd is, what it does, and why it matters.
3. They take one meaningful action.
4. The action is captured correctly with consent and appropriate privacy controls.
5. Asc3nd staff can see it and follow up without developer intervention.
6. The source, status, and resulting engagement can be measured.

## Valid meaningful actions

At least one of these may serve as the first verified loop:

- youth/program interest;
- parent/family inquiry;
- volunteer interest;
- mentor interest;
- donor/sponsor inquiry;
- partner inquiry;
- event participation.

The first loop should be selected based on the shortest safe path to a real, measurable outcome.

## Evidence required

A success claim requires evidence for the complete chain:

- public entry point;
- CTA/action taken;
- successful data persistence;
- staff-visible record or notification;
- documented follow-up capability;
- attribution/source captured;
- measurable result recorded.

A 200 response, green build, deployed page, or submitted form alone is not sufficient proof.

## Commercial value

This work creates value by:

- fulfilling a paid-client obligation;
- producing an external case study for PAULI STUDIO / MACS;
- proving a repeatable nonprofit acquisition/engagement architecture;
- reducing manual follow-up and lost inquiries;
- creating reusable patterns for future nonprofit clients.
