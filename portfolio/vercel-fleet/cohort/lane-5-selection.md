# Lane 5 — Dynamic selection rule

## Founder decision

Lane 5 remains dynamically reserved until the portfolio audit scores PAULI STUDIO-owned commercial assets by evidence rather than preference.

## Selection objective

Choose the existing owned asset with the strongest credible revenue-to-effort ratio and a realistic path to a paying customer, booking, sale, or recurring contract within 30–60 days.

## Required scoring dimensions

Each candidate must be scored on:

- time to first revenue;
- current completion / production readiness;
- clarity of buyer and problem;
- existing distribution access;
- recurring-revenue potential;
- commercial margin;
- founder attention required;
- remaining implementation complexity;
- ability to reuse existing systems rather than rebuild;
- evidence that demand already exists.

## Decision rule

Do not select Lane 5 because it is the most exciting, creative, technically interesting, or strategically ambitious project. Select it because the evidence shows it has the strongest near-term commercial return relative to remaining effort and founder attention.

## Gate

Status: `RESERVED_PENDING_PORTFOLIO_AUDIT`

No autonomous implementation should begin for Lane 5 until the scoring audit is complete and the founder selects from the ranked shortlist.
